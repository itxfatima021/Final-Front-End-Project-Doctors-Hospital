CEO Message
As-Salaam-Alaikum
It is with great pleasure, responsibility and in our earnest endeavor to put back “CARE” in healthcare, that I take this opportunity to introduce Doctors Hospital & Medical Center. Doctors Hospital & Medical Center is the culmination of hard work, passion, allegiance, and sacrifices of the hospital’s board, consultants, and employees to achieve the objectives of this project.
Pakistan has a long history of ignored healthcare systems and is grappling with a large and ever-increasing burden of health ailments. In 1996, a group of experienced doctors in Pennsylvania, United States came together with a vision of becoming a leading private healthcare provider in the country. These doctors triggered a reverse brain drain in the greater interest of the people of Pakistan and undertook the responsibility of moving back to Pakistan to build an institution that has set new trends in healthcare delivery in Pakistan. Today, with xx foreign-qualified consultants with decades of expertise, Doctors Hospital & Medical Center provides unmatched services in the health sector.
Our strategy at Doctors Hospital & Medical Center has always been to create an impact on Pakistan’s healthcare landscape by providing service at par with international standards, that is also accessible for wide strata of the society and cost-efficient. Starting in 2022, the hospital management and its employees have renewed their focus on three goals 1) patient-centered care, 2) a strong focus on patient safety and outcomes, and 3) being fair to the patient in their moment of need.
It is an honor for me to serve patients in Punjab and Pakistan through Doctors Hospital and Medical Center. We are resolute in our focus to diligently provide patients with the most comprehensive and compassionate health services. The hospital will continue its tradition of offering state-of-the-art clinical care, and the highest level of medical education and research, Insha’Allah!
May Allah SWT give us the strength and help us in our endeavors to support those who are sick and cannot take care of themselves.
Thank you for your support!
Dr. Muhammad Sarwar
Chief Executive Officer
Doctors Hospital & Medical Center
About Us
Founded in 2000, Doctors Hospital is an internationally renowned 250-bed hospital located in Lahore, Pakistan. For more than 18 years we have been fulfilling the medical needs of our community with our staff of American & British Board Certified specialists.
